# What is this file?
  Humans.txt is a text file that provides information about the
  people who have contributed to the creation of a website. It
  is a way for website owners to give credit to the individuals
  who have worked on the site and to provide contact information
  for those individuals.
  More info here: https://humanstxt.org/
